Training in the Vaganova tradition, examined by the Royal Academy of Dance, led by faculty formed at The Australian Ballet.

Jiayin Masterclass trains dancers the way the great schools always have: slowly, systematically, and with absolute attention to detail.

Founded in 2011 by Jiayin Du, formerly of The Australian Ballet and for seven years a classical teacher at The Australian Ballet School.

One enrolment. One year of formation.

One annual enrolment encompasses the complete curriculum: technique, pointe, character, conditioning and examination preparation, planned across the year as a single body of work.

There are no casual classes and no shortcuts. There is a method, and there is time. A scientific approach to health and injury prevention underpins every level.

Admissions

Admission is by consultation.

The school accepts a limited number of students each year, throughout the year. There are no trial classes; there is a conversation, a tour, and a class with the faculty.

  1. 01
    EnquiryA conversation about your child, your hopes, and the school.
  2. 02
    Studio TourBy appointment. See the studios, meet the school.
  3. 03
    Placement ClassThe faculty meets your dancer and advises the level.

    OfferA place in the formation, enrolled for the year.
